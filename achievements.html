Campus Reporting Award

Best Campus News Coverage

This recognition honors the publication's commitment to clear, timely, and well-sourced campus reporting. Through consistent coverage of school events, student initiatives, and administrative developments, The Root Lens demonstrated how student journalism can keep a community informed with accuracy and purpose.

The award also reflects the editorial team's discipline in verifying facts, organizing information, and presenting stories in a way that remains accessible to readers. More than a title, it represents the publication's role as a trusted school voice.

Event Coverage Feature

Photobooth Feature Showcase

This achievement highlights The Root Lens' creative coverage of a school photobooth experience that brought students together during a special campus event. Through strong visuals and engaging storytelling, the feature preserved the excitement, expressions, and atmosphere of the moment.

It recognizes the publication's ability to turn a simple activity into a meaningful campus memory, showing how student media can document joy, participation, and school culture in a way that readers can revisit and appreciate.

Event Reporting Spotlight

Cluster Meet Coverage Highlight

This achievement recognizes The Root Lens for documenting the cluster meet with energy, clarity, and strong campus reporting. The feature highlighted the spirit of competition, teamwork, and pride shown by students as they represented the school in different activities.

It celebrates the publication's ability to turn a major school event into a lasting story, preserving the moments, performances, and emotions that made the cluster meet meaningful for both participants and readers.

Division Press Conference Tribute

2025 DSPC Recognition

This achievement honors all DSDSNHS representatives in the 2025 Division Schools Press Conference for giving their best in representing the school. It also recognizes the unwavering support of the Dakudanian community, whose encouragement helped strengthen the team throughout the competition.

As shared by proud coach and moderator Mrs. Tomenio, the team showed dedication, spirit, passion for journalism, and strong support for one another.

Campus Celebration Feature

Valentine Crowning Spotlight

This achievement highlights The Root Lens' feature on the Mr. and Ms. Valentine crowning, where the celebration continued with warm congratulations, resounding cheers, and shared memories across the school community.

The publication also proudly recognized its own staff who participated and represented The Root Lens with excellence, making the event an especially proud milestone for the organization.
