Why Read The Root Lens?

A closer look at the publication's purpose, voice, and role in keeping the school community informed.

  • Campus News
  • Student Features
  • Creative Voices

Campus News That Matters

The Root Lens delivers timely updates on school programs, student achievements, campus events, and announcements that shape everyday school life.

It gives readers one trusted space to stay informed, aware, and connected to what is happening across the community.

Stories Behind the Headlines

Beyond announcements, the publication highlights student experiences, inspiring journeys, and meaningful interviews that bring depth to every issue.

These features celebrate the people, values, and moments that make the school community unique.

A Home for Student Expression

The Root Lens opens space for editorials, opinion pieces, poetry, artwork, and other creative contributions that reflect student insight and imagination.

It is not only a source of information, but also a platform where student voices can be seen, heard, and remembered.
